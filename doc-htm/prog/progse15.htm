5.4 Supported MMX operations

The following operations are supported in the compiler when MMX extensions are enabled:

  • addition (+)
  • subtraction (-)
  • multiplication(*)
  • logical exclusive or (xor)
  • logical and (and)
  • logical or (or)
  • sign change (-)
